# Games Portfolio

A portfolio of my latest web games.

Live at [games.foxpress.io](https://games.foxpress.io)

## Games

### 🚀 Spaceless
A space survival idle clicker. Flee a dead Earth, scavenge orbital debris, and bootstrap your way across the solar system. Play at [games.foxpress.io/spaceless](https://games.foxpress.io/spaceless/).

### 🪨 Mancala
A classic board game brought online with real-time multiplayer. Play at [mancala.foxpress.io](https://mancala.foxpress.io).

### 🧠 Convergence
A multiplayer word game where everyone picks a secret starting word, then takes turns trying to meet in the middle. Play at [convergence.foxpress.io](https://convergence.foxpress.io).
